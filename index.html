Product

Vast Visions are sleek AR glasses that overlay apps, navigation and calls into your field of view. Works with iOS, Android, macOS and Windows.

Display: micro-OLED, 55° FOV
Input: voice, eye-tracking, pinch
Audio: open-ear + beamforming mics
